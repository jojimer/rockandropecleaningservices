Making High Angle Simple

What We Do?

ROCK AND ROPE CLEANING SERVICES is a modern building maintenance that employs safe and fast professional window cleaning - making possible working at height very simple. We access building exteriors via specialized Twin Rope Access (TRA), which utilizes two rope descent systems in cleaning glass windows thus keeping us uniquely apart from the traditional method of providing window cleaning services.

What We Offer?

We offer window cleaning services for high-rise, low-rise, and commercial buildings throughout the Philippines. Our additional services include Paint and Sealant Job, Rectification Works, Building Inspection, Repair and Maintenance. Our shared experience cleaning windows on some of the most difficult skyscrapers in Metro Manila makes us uniquely qualified to provide the best window cleaning service.

About Us

We specialize primarily in rope suspension work on high-rise and commercial window cleaning. Our team is composed of fully trained and insured rope access technicians who are dedicated to safety, compliance, and your complete satisfaction. By using rope access abseil window cleaning, we can gain access to places where other cleaning window companies fear to tread, at the same time avoiding costly scaffoldings and the hassle of other fixed structure. It will help not only reduce costs but also the time involved being much quicker setting up the rope abseiling equipment than other window cleaning methods.

High-rise and commercial buildings window cleaning is a high-risk work! Let the professionals at Rock And Rope Cleaning Services make your windows look like new with our safe, fast, expedient, and cost-effective window cleaning services. Trust a professional and reliable window cleaning company to do the job right and provide your building the best cleaning possible.

Rock And Rope Cleaning Services is continually striving to uphold excellence, quality, and value. Our main goal is to provide unsurpassed quality window cleaning. Our sparkling clean windows, significantly enhance the natural beauty of the buildings we service and are an essential element in the professional image projected by our business clients.

We understand that your complete satisfaction is the key to our on-going success, all work performed will be completed as specified in a professional and workmanlike manner, following the BS 7985 Code of practice for the use of rope access methods for industrial purposes, and complying with safety rules and regulations.
